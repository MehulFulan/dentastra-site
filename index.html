Our RCM Workflow

Step 1: Patient Onboarding

Gather and verify patient demographics, insurance details, and consent forms digitally.

Step 2: Eligibility Verification

Confirm benefits, coverage limits, and co-payment responsibilities before the visit.

Step 3: Treatment Planning

Ensure accurate coding and obtain pre-authorizations for planned dental procedures.

Step 4: Claim Submission

Submit clean claims electronically with error-checking logic to minimize rejections.

Step 5: Payment Reconciliation

Match remittances to claims and patient accounts with zero-tolerance for variance.

Step 6: Denial Appeals

Resolve denied claims by analyzing root causes and submitting timely corrections.

Step 7: Collections Follow-Up

Send patient statements and follow-up reminders for any outstanding balances.

Step 8: Monthly Reporting

Review KPI dashboards like Net Collections, A/R aging, and claim turnaround times.
